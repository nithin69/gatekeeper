What is aoucr?

aoucr provides relevant, reliable and comprehensive information about government organizations, business units and latest updates in vizag city. It is an innovation platform for the the Greater Visakhapatnam Area. It collaborates with businesses, residents, the municipality and knowledge institutions to suggest and apply innovative ideas & solutions for urban issues. aoucr has grown into a platform comprising of 50 partners, who are actively involved in more than 20 innovative projects.


How can you contribute?

aoucr is keen to work closely with city residents and visitors of Vizag to develop ideas and solutions aimed at improving the liveability of the city. After all, they know what they like, what does (and doesn’t) work and how they envisage the future for their own house, their street, neighbourhood, and perhaps even the entire city. If you’ve got an idea or solution, we’d love to hear it! .


Why is this important?

aoucr believes in a liveable city where people can live and work pleasantly. It connects people in order to enable the creation of smart collaborations and solutions. This contributes to the liveability of the Vizag, promotes sustainable economic growth and helps develop new markets..
