Tenneti ParK




Description

Tenneti Park on the Vizag-Bheemili Road in Vishakapatnam, Andhra Pradesh is a beautiful park from where the sea and the sunrise views are popular. This is one destination which tourists who visit Vishakapatnam do not miss because the little park is simply remarkable and is set in a picturesque location facing the sea. The combination of multicolored hues of the verdant park, the azure blue water and the pristine skies and the kaleidoscopic flora in the park along with well laid walkways, pebbles and rock landscaping make for wonderful scenery, right out of a picture book. The hills forming the backdrop to this setting only add to the allure. Away from the bustling city, Tenneti Park attracts a lot of residents looking for respite from the hustle and bustle of city life, who come and picnic here with their friends and family.
